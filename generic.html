Get to know us!

Dhruv Aggarwal

Dhruv is one of our co-founder's who is currently a Junior in Computer Engineering at Purdue. He is from Edison, New Jersey and is passionate about coding. His experience consists of working for Valiance Partners and doing software engineering for them. Along with that, he studied abroad in Rome taking a machine learning and pattern recognition class. He also has done research in aerial robotics and loves to give back to the community.

Dhruv loves airplanes and aviation and could talk to you about it all day. He also loves sports and his favorite teams are the NY Yankees and Portland TrailBlazers. He is quite outgoing so feel free to contact him at aggarw45@purdue.edu. As co-founder, Dhruv's goal is to get Flyr in the hands of as many college students as possible and help as many students as he can!
